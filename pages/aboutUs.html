About Us

In 1862, Stephen Taylor and Anthony Harrow were two wealth managers in Chicago's metropolis area and had found the services industry lacked the quality schemes they had previously encountered.

They set out with one mission - to envigourate a failing commonwealth and shake up aspects from inside

With this in mind they established Taylor & Harrow Associates three years later in 1865 occupying a small office in the Old Fruit Exchange Square.

Some 150 years later, Taylor & Harrow are now one of the most established practices in the midwest, offering exceptional service to clients.
